# Visual Style Guide for Soulfables

Your cinematic blueprint for creating emotionally resonant video content.

## 🎬 BEST CLIP CHOICES (RANKED)

### 🥇 1. "Alone in a Crowd / Disconnected Person" (TOP CHOICE)

**What it looks like:**
- A person sitting or standing in a public place (train, street, café)
- Everyone moving normally around them
- The main character looks still, distant, or unfocused

**👉 Why it works:**
Visually represents: *"He was there… but not really there."*

This is the **strongest match** for your Soulfables script.

---

### 🥈 2. "Walking Alone at Night / City Lights Blur"

**What it looks like:**
- Slow walking shot
- Neon lights or streetlights
- Slight motion blur or shallow focus

**👉 Why it works:**
Perfect for: *emotional drift + identity confusion*

---

### 🥉 3. "Mirror Reflection / Staring at Self"

**What it looks like:**
- Person looking into a mirror
- Slight hesitation or blank expression
- Soft lighting, cinematic tone

**👉 Why it works:**
Perfect for: *"he didn't recognize himself anymore"*

---

## 🔥 BEST VISUAL SEQUENCE (Multi-Clip Strategy)

Use this narrative arc across your video:

| Timeline | Visual | Purpose |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Hook (0–3s) | Crowd / alone in public | Establish isolation |
| Middle | Walking alone at night | Deepen emotional drift |
| Crisis | Mirror reflection | Peak moment of realization |
| Ending | Still shot, slow fade out | Resolution + reflection |

---

## ⚡ SOULFABLES BRAND VISUAL RULES

### ❌ What NOT to use:
- Action-heavy clips
- Fast cuts early in video
- Bright energetic scenes
- Distracting backgrounds
- Multiple characters

### ✅ What TO use:
- **Quiet emotional realism**
- Consistent single character (preferably Black character matching your voice)
- Soft, moody lighting
- Slow deliberate movements
- Cinematic depth of field
- Minimal distractions

---

## 🎨 CINEMATOGRAPHY SETTINGS (For Runway AI)

When generating visuals in Runway, use:

**Prompt Style:**
```
"Close-up of a Black man sitting alone in a crowded public space, 
cinematic lighting, slightly out of focus background, 
emotional introspection, photorealistic, 4K"
```

**Key Elements:**
- Consistent character (same person throughout)
- Soft/moody color grading
- Shallow depth of field
- Slow, deliberate framing
- Empty space around subject
- Minimal context clues

---

## 📏 VISUAL PACING

- **0–3 sec:** Hook clip (establishes emotion instantly)
- **3–8 sec:** Building clip (deepens the mood)
- **8–12 sec:** Crisis clip (peak emotional moment)
- **12–15 sec:** Resolution clip (slow fade to black)

**👉 Rule:** Every 2–3 seconds = visual change (zoom, cut, or transition)

---

## 🎬 EDITING GUIDELINES (CapCut)

1. **Slow zooms** (0.5x–1.5x over 2–3 sec)
2. **Fade cuts** (0.3–0.5 sec transitions)
3. **Subtle glitch** (optional, very minimal)
4. **Blur transitions** (when shifting between scenes)
5. **No quick cuts** (breaks emotional tension)

---

## 💡 PRO TIP: CHARACTER CONSISTENCY

Use the same character throughout your video. This:
- Builds emotional connection
- Makes the story feel personal
- Increases retention (viewers track the "character")
- Creates brand recognition over time

When using Runway, specify: *"same character as before"* or provide character reference.
