# he-wasnt-the-same-anymore

## HOOK (0–3s)
He thought he was okay again…

(pause)

## SITUATION
Life looked normal on the outside.
He still showed up.
Still smiled.
Still said all the right things.

(pause)

## SHIFT
But something felt off.
Not loud. Not dramatic.
Just… different.

Like he was watching himself instead of living.

(pause)

## CRISIS
Days started blending together.
Moments didn't feel real anymore.
Even silence felt heavy.

And for a second…
he thought he was losing himself again.

(pause)

## REALIZATION
It wasn't that he went backward.
It was that healing doesn't move in a straight line.

Some days you feel whole.
Some days you don't recognize yourself.

(pause)

## PAYOFF
And that doesn't mean you're broken.
It just means you're still becoming.

(pause)

## CTA (soft)
If this felt familiar…
you're not the only one going through it.
