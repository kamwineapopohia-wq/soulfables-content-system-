# Daily Content Workflow

Your step-by-step system to create emotionally resonant content that converts viewers into engaged followers.

## 🌅 1. IDEA SEED (5–10 min)

Pick ONE source for your idea:

- A personal feeling ("I felt disconnected today…")
- A universal emotion (failure, regret, hope, identity loss)
- A viral hook from your library
- A comment/reply from audience
- A "what if…" scenario

**👉 Output:** One sentence idea only

**Example:** "What if someone slowly stopped recognizing themselves?"

---

## 🎯 2. HOOK SELECTION (2 min)

Go to `/hooks/viral-hooks.md` and pick ONE.

**Or use this formula:**
- "He didn't realize it at first…"
- "Nobody noticed it, but something changed…"
- "This is how people lose themselves…"

**👉 Rule:** If the hook doesn't create curiosity in 2 seconds, scrap it.

---

## 🧠 3. SCRIPT BUILD (10–15 min)

Use `/templates/story-template.md` structure:

```
HOOK (0–3s)
[Curiosity + tension]

(pause)

SITUATION
[Normal life setup]

(pause)

SHIFT
[Something feels off]

(pause)

CRISIS
[Emotional low point]

(pause)

REALIZATION
[What it means emotionally]

(pause)

PAYOFF
[Insight / truth]

CTA (soft)
["If this felt familiar…" / "You know this feeling…"]
```

**👉 Rule:** Each section = 1 emotional idea only.

---

## 🎙️ 4. VOICE GENERATION (5–10 min)

Use ElevenLabs:

1. Paste script
2. Settings:
   - Stability: 40–55%
   - Style exaggeration: 60–75%
   - Speed: slightly slow (0.9x–1.0x)

**👉 Goal:** emotional, not robotic

---

## 🎬 5. VIDEO CREATION (Runway + CapCut) (20–40 min)

**Workflow:**
1. Generate visuals in Runway (cinematic, emotional, abstract human scenes)
2. Import into CapCut
3. Sync voiceover
4. Add:
   - Slow zooms
   - Fade cuts
   - Subtle glitch or blur transitions

**👉 Rule:** Every 2–3 seconds = visual change

---

## ✍️ 6. CAPTIONS + POSTING TEXT (5 min)

**Instagram / Facebook:**
- Emotional one-liner
- 1 reflection sentence
- CTA question

**TikTok / YouTube Shorts:**
- Hook sentence only + curiosity

**Example:**
```
"He didn't realize he was disappearing…"
```

---

## 🧲 7. POSTING CHECKLIST (FINAL)

Before posting, confirm:

- ✔ Hook creates curiosity in <2 sec
- ✔ Emotion is clear (loss / identity / tension)
- ✔ No long explanations early
- ✔ Visual changes every few seconds
- ✔ Ending feels reflective, not "salesy"

---

## 📊 8. AFTER POST (MOST IMPORTANT STEP)

After 2–4 hours, log:

- Views
- Likes
- Retention feeling (did it feel slow or engaging?)
- Best moment (where people likely stayed)

**Put into:** `/analytics/post-performance.md`
