# Runway AI Visual Generation Prompts

Your complete visual blueprint for creating emotionally resonant cinematic clips.

---

## 🎞️ CLIP 1 — HOOK (Emotional Disconnection in Public)

**Duration:** 3–4 seconds

**Prompt:**

```
Cinematic shot of a young Black man sitting alone in a crowded urban 
environment, people walking past in fast motion while he remains completely 
still, emotional isolation in a busy world, shallow depth of field, soft 
natural lighting, muted color grading, subtle film grain, slow cinematic 
push-in toward subject, realistic human expression, 4k ultra cinematic 
realism, feeling of disconnection and internal emptiness
```

**Visual Notes:**
- Don't show full face immediately (creates curiosity)
- Focus on posture: still while world moves
- Muted colors: greys, blues, warm minimal accents
- This is your hook moment—let ambiguity draw viewers in

---

## 🎞️ CLIP 2 — SHIFT (Walking Alone, Emotional Drift)

**Duration:** 3–4 seconds

**Prompt:**

```
A lone figure of a young Black man walking slowly down a quiet city street 
at night, neon lights reflecting on wet pavement, soft cinematic lighting, 
shallow depth of field, blurred background city movement, slow handheld 
camera motion, introspective mood, emotional loneliness, subtle motion blur, 
cinematic color grading with cool blue and warm orange tones, realistic 
atmosphere, 4k film style
```

**Visual Notes:**
- Slow, deliberate movement (no rushing)
- Night setting deepens emotional tone
- Neon reflection = visual metaphor for searching
- Shallow focus keeps attention on character

---

## 🎞️ CLIP 3 — CRISIS (Identity Confusion Mirror Scene)

**Duration:** 4–5 seconds

**Prompt:**

```
Close-up cinematic shot of a young Black man standing in front of a mirror 
in a dimly lit room, staring at his reflection with a blank and disconnected 
expression, slow zoom-in toward face, soft shadows across skin, shallow 
depth of field, emotional tension, muted tones, subtle film grain, realistic 
lighting, feeling of identity confusion and emotional distance, 4k cinematic 
realism
```

**Visual Notes:**
- This is your peak emotional moment
- Zoom-in emphasizes introspection
- Blank expression > theatrical acting
- Shadows create visual depth and tension
- This is where full face reveal happens

---

## 🎞️ CLIP 4 — REALIZATION (Calm Resolution / Acceptance)

**Duration:** 3–4 seconds

**Prompt:**

```
A still cinematic shot of a young Black man sitting near a window with soft 
morning light streaming in, dust particles visible in the light beams, calm 
and reflective atmosphere, minimal movement, warm muted tones, shallow depth 
of field, slow fade-in feeling, emotional resolution and quiet acceptance, 
cinematic realism, 4k film look
```

**Visual Notes:**
- Shift from night → morning (visual metaphor for acceptance)
- Light beams = hope without being obvious
- Stillness = peace after drift
- Warm tones (final shift from cool blues)
- Ending feeling should be open, not conclusive

## 🎬 PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

### 🧠 1. SCRIPT ALIGNMENT
- ✓ Hook matches Clip 1 emotion
- ✓ Emotional escalation follows clips 1 → 4
- ✓ No visual mismatch with narration
- ✓ Pauses in script sync with scene transitions

### 🎥 2. CLIP GENERATION (Runway Settings)
- ✓ Generate each clip at 4–6 seconds
- ✓ Use **consistent character appearance** across all clips (same person)
- ✓ Avoid fast action scenes
- ✓ Prioritize **facial emotion** over movement
- ✓ Request "same character" in follow-up generations

### 🎧 3. AUDIO SETUP (Voiceover)
- ✓ Use Soulfables voice preset (`VOICE-GENERATION-GUIDE.md`)
- ✓ Speed: 0.90x
- ✓ Align pauses with scene changes
- ✓ First line starts **BEFORE** visuals fully load (hook effect)
- ✓ Generate in ElevenLabs before editing

### ✂️ 4. EDITING (CapCut or Similar)
- ✓ Cut every 2–3 seconds max
- ✓ Add slow zoom-in on emotional moments
- ✓ Use soft fade transitions (no hard cuts early)
- ✓ Keep background music **low** (10–20% volume)
- ✓ Sync voiceover pauses to visual cuts

### 🎨 5. COLOR & MOOD
- ✓ Muted tones: blues, greys, warm accents
- ✓ Slight film grain overlay (cinematic feel)
- ✓ Avoid overly saturated visuals
- ✓ Consistency across all 4 clips

### 📱 6. FINAL VIRAL CHECK
- ✓ Hook creates curiosity in first 2 seconds ✓
- ✓ Viewer understands emotion **without audio**
- ✓ Ending feels open, not "finished"
- ✓ Visuals match emotional pacing
- ✓ No jarring transitions
- ✓ Character recognition across all scenes

## ⚡ PRO TIPS (IMPORTANT)

### Retention Boost
- Don't show full character face immediately in Clip 1
- Let Clip 1 be slightly unclear → curiosity boost
- Save full emotional reveal for Clip 3
- This creates a "story arc" visually

### Character Consistency
When generating Clip 2, 3, 4 in Runway:
- Always reference: "Same character as before"
- Use consistent lighting direction
- Maintain facial hair / appearance

### Audio-Visual Sync
- Voiceover pause = visual cut
- Emotional line = close-up zoom
- Silence = hold on a moment
- CTA = fade to black
